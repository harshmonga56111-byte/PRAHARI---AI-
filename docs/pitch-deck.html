Detection · Engines 01 & 02
📞Digital Arrest Shield
A trained TF-IDF + Logistic Regression classifier fused with a transparent rule layer. Flags active call/SMS/WhatsApp sessions to telecom providers and potential victims before the financial transfer completes.
TF-IDF + LogReg 10 indicator families explainable verdicts auto telecom/1930 alert
💵Counterfeit Agent
Computer vision deployable on phones, counting machines, and PoS terminals. Verifies micro-print, security thread, colour, and serial across all 7 denominations — returning a per-feature authenticity report.
micro-print energy security-thread band RBI serial validation no GPU required
Disruption & Response · Engines 03 · 04 · 05

From transaction metadata to a patrol on the street.

🕸️ FRAUD GRAPH
NetworkX community detection + PageRank over money-flow, device, and phone linkages. Clusters mules and controllers into rings, then exports an evidence package where every edge maps to a timestamped transaction record.
🗺️ GEO INTEL
Grid-based hotspot clustering of complaints and seizures. Scores intensity, prioritises patrol deployment, and enables inter-district intelligence sharing through a command-center map.
🛡️ CITIZEN SHIELD
Conversational risk assessment on WhatsApp, IVR, and app. Instant verdicts, guided 1930/NCRB reporting, advisory in 12 languages — tuned for a very low false-positive rate.
